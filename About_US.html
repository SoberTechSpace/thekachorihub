The kachori hub- The name itself will share about our brand.

Kachori, a well known & popular street food item across India.It is popular among the Indians but overall it has not received extreme fame by us. As the product is simple, looks delicious & fulfills your hunger: So a simple formula for your satisfaction. So as to provide kachori the fame it deserves, we at The kachori hub are offering the best kachoris in the market to our customers.

Yes, We maintain hygiene..!!!

We maintain a high level of hygiene at our shop & with internal operations as well. All our helpers, chefs & vendors follow the daily screening process. So hygiene is our top priority.

Our journey-

We have been primarily in the battery products business for many years. It was a big shift for us in terms of our shop, training our resources for this new world, letting our customers know about our new business without losing our old business. With the aim of serving a variety of kachori products, we wanted to explore new opportunities. Pune is a place for foodies, where street food is loved and is open to taste new products. From classic Kachori to Spinach Corn Kachori. Foodies will get to taste these flavours which were never even imagined when it comes to kachori only at The Kachori Hub. And this is just the beginning….!!!!
